About Lavender Paw Soak

Our Lavender Paw Soak is a gentle, effective solution for cleaning and soothing your dog's paws after walks or outdoor activities. Formulated with natural ingredients and essential oils, this soak helps remove dirt, allergens, and irritants while moisturizing dry, cracked paw pads.

The calming lavender scent helps reduce anxiety during grooming sessions, making it easier to care for your pet's paws. Regular use can help maintain healthy paws and prevent issues caused by environmental irritants.

Benefits:

  • Cleans and removes dirt, salt, and allergens from paws
  • Soothes and moisturizes dry, cracked paw pads
  • Calming lavender scent reduces anxiety during grooming
  • Helps prevent irritation from environmental factors
  • Made with 100% natural, pet-safe ingredients
  • Vet-approved formula
  • No harsh chemicals, parabens, or artificial fragrances

How to Use

1

Prepare

Fill a shallow container with warm water and add 1-2 tablespoons of Lavender Paw Soak.

2

Soak

Gently place your dog's paw in the solution and let it soak for 1-2 minutes. Repeat with each paw.

3

Clean

Use your fingers or a soft cloth to gently clean between paw pads and around nails.

4

Rinse

Rinse paws with clean water to remove any remaining solution.

5

Dry

Pat dry with a clean towel. Follow with Pawlor Paw Balm for extra protection if needed.

Pro Tips:

  • Use after walks, especially in urban areas or during allergy season
  • For best results, use 2-3 times per week
  • Can be used as a preventative measure before walks in snowy or icy conditions
  • Store at room temperature away from direct sunlight
